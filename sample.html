Skip the coffee shop queues with barista creations at home
 
Dreaming of an iced latte to kickstart a sunny morning? Or a steamy cappuccino to get you through a work call? With these clever devices, you can enjoy your favourite drinks without leaving your home.

With so many types to choose from, the coffee-tunities are endless
 
 
Bean to cup
 
We can't physically bring your favourite coffee house to you, but this is the next best thing. Bean to cup machines are like having your very own barista - grinding the beans from scratch for the freshest flat white in town. Some models even let you save your favourite drinks to a menu, so your usual is ready to go at the touch of a button.
 
Shop now
 
Espresso
 
This serious bit of kit uses ground coffee to make an intense shot of espresso right before your eyes. Or, opt for one with a built-in grinder so you can use beans fresh from the bag. A lot of these models come with steam arms or milk frothers too, meaning you can transform your humble espresso shot into a silky-smooth cappuccino or latte.
 
Shop now
 
Filter
 
Need copious amounts of coffee to ease you into your mornings? We're right there with you. Filter machines can make up to 12 cups of coffee at a time and can keep it warm for up to half an hour - ideal for busy days at the office or catch-ups with friends. All they need is ground coffee and water, making them easy to use and great value for money.
 
Shop now
 
Pod
 
For a quick and easy way to soothe those caffeine cravings, go for a pod coffee machine. They filter water through specially-designed capsules, so all you have to do is press and go. Not only are they easy to use, but there's a huge range of drinks available too - from your morning Americano to a decadent hot chocolate before bed. Bliss.
 
Shop now
